Welcome to Discover!

Dubai is the most populous city in the United Arab Emirates (UAE) and the capital of the Emirate of Dubai. Located on the southeast coast of the Persian Gulf, Dubai is a global city and business hub of the Middle East. The city has a diverse population of around 3.3 million people and is known for its luxurious shopping, modern architecture, and vibrant nightlife. In recent years, Dubai has become a popular tourist destination, attracting millions of visitors each year.

Demographic Data

  • Population: 3,331,000 (2021)
  • Area: 1,588.4 km²
  • Density: 2,100/km²
  • Language: Arabic (official), English, Hindi, Urdu
  • Currency: United Arab Emirates dirham (AED)
  • Time zone: Gulf Standard Time (UTC+4)
