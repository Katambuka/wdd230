News Release

Dubai Chamber of Commerce introduces Business Sectors Platform to Boost Efficiency and Impact Dubai Chamber of Commerce, one of the three Chambers operating under Dubai Chambers, has launched the Business Sectors Platform to provide value-added services to Business Groups and Business Councils. The platform aims to enhance Dubai's reputation as a global business and trade hub by offering tailored services to meet the needs of these entities. The launch event was attended by over 200 heads and members of Business Groups and Business Councils. The platform will enhance the competitiveness of these groups, support Dubai's digital transformation agenda, and enable two-way communication between the public and private sectors. It features specialized services, including the Digital Presence Programme and Advocacy Programme, among others. The event also introduced the new Business Advocacy Sector, which streamlines advocacy efforts to drive business and boost economic competitiveness. Business Groups and Business Councils play a vital role in Dubai's business landscape, representing different sectors and facilitating dialogue between stakeholders and government entities.
